# AI Policy

There are no strict rules, and currently no stance on whether or not AI-assisted
contributions are prohibited or welcome. There are, however, some guidelines based
on personal experience.

## You should understand the changes you are submitting

This section should be self-evident, and doesn't require elaboration.

## The best AI code is not noticeably AI at all

There should always be some human review *before* a contribution is submitted. If your
contribution is noticeably AI-generated, that likely means that you have not properly
reviewed and cleaned up the contribution. When the contributor does not review their
own contribution, this becomes what some may call spam or slop.

If you are merely passing off issues or review feedback to an AI agent to address, then
why are *you* doing it when anyone else with access to an AI agent can? The purpose of
this question isn't to shame you for using AI, but to encourage you to add your own
knowledge or expertise to your contribution.
